Cantor College

Your future starts here

Cantor College provides a perfect balance between academic success and a vibrant social life.



Cantor College was established in 1989 to specialize in Computing and Design. We aim to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and experience to succeed in their careers. Our students have gone on to have successful careers across various industries worldwide!

What We Represent

We Ignite Imagination

Creativity isn’t confined to one discipline; it’s a way of thinking. We’ll inspire you to explore various fields, mediums, cultures, and traditions. You'll get expert teaching from active practitioners and researchers who will encourage you to adopt innovative and resourceful approaches that both perceive and create opportunities for better lives.

We Expand Horizons

We take pride in our diverse culture and global connections, empowering our graduates to thrive anywhere. The College teaches undergraduate and postgraduate courses in a wide range of specialisms, including computer science, software development, information systems, networking and software engineering. It is at the heart of a passionate computing community, including student societies devoted to games development, digital forensics and programming.

We Celebrate Uniqueness

We’re here to help you shape an experience that reflects your interests and dreams, both during your time here and in your future career. You’ll develop your creative practice whilst interacting with your peers, as well as learning professional skills by working on applied briefs facilitated through our links with diverse institutions.
