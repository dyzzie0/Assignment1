Facilities

  • • Advanced Computing Labs: Equipped with the latest hardware and software for programming, cybersecurity, data science, and more, accessible 24/7.
  • • Design Studios: High-end graphic tablets, professional-grade software, and large-format printers for graphic design, digital media, and product design.
  • • Makerspace: A hub for creativity with 3D printers, laser cutters, CNC machines, and advanced prototyping tools.
  • • Technology Sandbox: An experimental space for exploring the latest in VR, AR, and AI technologies.
  • • Collaborative Learning Spaces: Designed to foster teamwork and creative problem-solving with smartboards and flexible seating.
  • • Student Hub: The social heart of our campus with a café, lounge areas, and recreational facilities.
  • • Career and Development Centre: Dedicated to helping you plan your future with career counseling and job placement services.
  • • Fitness and Wellness Centre: A fully equipped gym and wellness services including yoga and counseling.
  • • On-Campus Housing: Modern and comfortable housing options with community amenities.
  • • Outdoor Spaces: Beautiful landscaped gardens and outdoor seating areas for relaxation.
  • • Accessibility Services: Support and accommodations for students with disabilities, ensuring equal access to education.
